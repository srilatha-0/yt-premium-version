What’s included with YouTube Premium?

YouTube Premium includes ad-free access to YouTube and YouTube Music, offline downloads, and background play.

How do I download videos and music?

You can download and watch videos and music on your mobile device using the YouTube app, the YouTube Music app, or the YouTube Kids app. You can also download videos and watch on your computer using Chrome, Edge & Opera browsers. You can watch or listen to content offline for up to 30 days without being connected to the internet.

How do I add people to my plan?

You can add members through the Purchases and Memberships page by clicking on edit next to Family Sharing settings. You can share your YouTube Premium membership with up to one additional household member with a Two-person plan and 5 additional household members with a Family plan. Once you get a plan, you can add members if you’re the family manager of your plan. Family members sharing a plan must live in the same household as the family manager and have a Google account. Each family member gets their own private, personalized account.
